Form vs Function

For my latest project, I created a video exploring the concept of "form vs function". To make the video engaging and informative, I used Blender 3D software to create custom visuals that would help viewers better understand the topic. To add an extra layer of interest to the video, I incorporated audio from another YouTube video on the same subject. I then added custom sound design throughout the video to enhance the viewer's experience. To give the video a polished and professional feel, I used my skills in animation and editing to transform the source material into an explainer video in the style of VOX, a popular YouTube channel known for its high-quality content. Overall, I'm really proud of the finished product. Through my technical abilities and attention to detail, I was able to take a complex concept and turn it into an engaging and accessible video that I believe will be valuable to anyone interested in the topic.
